## Full description (≤4000 chars)
KTU One is the all-in-one companion for APJ Abdul Kalam Technological University
(KTU) students. Check your results, crunch your grades, and keep up with
everything academic — in one clean, fast app.

**Results & profile**
- View your semester results and profile pulled straight from KTU.
- Track your CGPA and credits earned at a glance.

**Smart calculators**
- SGPA, CGPA, attendance, internal-marks, and pass-mark calculators.
- Pre-filled with your real grades when you're logged in.
- Saved history so you can revisit past calculations.

**Study resources**
- Browse and search question papers by branch, semester, and subject.
- Access syllabi and exam timetables.
- Bookmark what matters for quick access.

**Stay informed**
- Latest KTU notices across categories.
- Academic calendar with exams, holidays, results, and deadlines.

**Made for students**
- Clean, distraction-free notebook-inspired design.
- Light and dark themes.
- Works smoothly on mobile.

Support the app by becoming a Supporter to remove ads and back development.

_KTU One is an independent student utility and is not affiliated with or
endorsed by APJ Abdul Kalam Technological University._
