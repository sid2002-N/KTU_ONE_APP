# Privacy Policy — KTU One

_Last updated: 2026-07-01_

KTU One ("the app", "we", "us") helps APJ Abdul Kalam Technological University
(KTU) students view their results, calculate SGPA/CGPA, and browse question
papers, syllabi, notices, and the academic calendar. This policy explains what
data we handle and why.

## Information we collect

- **KTU credentials & register number.** When you log in, your KTU register
  number and password are sent to our backend, which authenticates against the
  official KTU portal on your behalf. We do **not** store your KTU password.
- **Academic data.** Your results, grades, CGPA, and profile details retrieved
  from KTU are cached on our servers and your device to display them to you.
- **App data you create.** Bookmarks and calculator history you save while
  logged in are stored so they sync across sessions.
- **Payment information.** If you become a Supporter, payments are processed by
  **Razorpay**. We do not receive or store your card/UPI details; we store only
  a record that the purchase succeeded.
- **Technical data.** Standard request metadata (e.g. IP address, timestamps)
  is processed transiently for security and rate limiting.

## How we use it

- To authenticate you and display your KTU results and profile.
- To provide app features (calculators, bookmarks, notices, calendar, papers).
- To process Supporter purchases.
- To protect the service (rate limiting, abuse prevention).

We do **not** sell your data or share it with third parties for advertising.
Anonymous, non-identifying ads may be shown to non-Supporter users.

## Data sharing

- **KTU portal** — credentials are relayed to authenticate you.
- **Razorpay** — payment processing (their privacy policy applies).
- **Infrastructure providers** — hosting, database, and storage vendors that
  operate the backend under contract.

## Data retention & deletion

- Session tokens expire automatically; logging out revokes them.
- Cached academic data is refreshed/expired on a rolling basis.
- To request deletion of your stored data, contact us at
  **<support@your-domain>** and we will remove it.

## Security

Data is transmitted over HTTPS. Authentication uses signed, httpOnly session
cookies. We take reasonable measures to protect your information, but no method
of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

## Children

The app is intended for university students and the general public, not for
children under 13.
